Hello There!


My name's Evan, and I'm a graduate of General Assembly's Web Development Intensive. While at General Assembly I spent every single day for 3 coding. While it was amongst the toughest challenges I've ever put myself against, it has by far been one of the most rewarding. During my time at General Assembly I studied Ruby, Rails, Git, JavaScript, jQuery, Sinatra, Sequel, Postgresql, Redis, HTML, CSS, Sass, RSPEC, Capybara, TTD, Agile Development, along with many other languages and concepts. I worked on numerous project sprints both solo and in groups, partook in many pair programming sessions, and administered github repos for group projects and assignments. All in all I'm a dedicated hard working individual who is easy to get along with, knows how to communicate, and keeps it real.
